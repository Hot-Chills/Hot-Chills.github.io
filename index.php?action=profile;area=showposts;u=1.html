News and Announcements / HC => Fws
« on: November 25, 2017, 22:35:18 »
After discussions with Fws we have decided to let Fws take over HC // Fun. It was a difficult decision for me since the server has been around for five years now. The shrinking HC community and my lack of time finally tipped the scale.

My hope is that HC // Fun will live on under its new name -[|Fws|]- HC // Fun and attract more players since it will be integrated into the bigger Fws community.

All pinned levels have been removed. Moderators and admins will have to re-apply at the Fws forum.

I want to thank everyone who has visited the server or the forum. A special thanks to the people that have volunteered as admins and moderators, helping new players and punishing cheaters. Without you this had not been possible!

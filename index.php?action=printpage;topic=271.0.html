Title: New moderator feature
Post by: Häppy C@mper on September 16, 2013, 21:18:40
A new feature for moderators has been added to the script. It's the possibility to identify and ban players after they have left the server. It works with menu commands only.

The players that have left the server are listed at the end of the player selection menu. Their IDs start at 33, 33 being the player that left most recently.

The new script will be activated soon.

Title: Re: New moderator feature
Post by: Häppy C@mper on September 17, 2013, 19:38:25
It's not a new command. When you open the administration and moderation menu (F3) and choose Check->Identify or Remove->Ban IP/U.S.G.N. or Name, the recent players are listed at the end of the players list. You can select them and identify or ban them.

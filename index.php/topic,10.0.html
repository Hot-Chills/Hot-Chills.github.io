The type of round I came up with is this:

Normal, full map, both terrorist and counter terrorist spawn at their regular spots. The weapon in use would be
-Tactical Shield
-Machete
-HE (unlimited)
The map would be filled with neutral (yellow) turrets, not too much so it won't slow the game (and lag) not too less so it wont be too easy (and therefore the point of the round would be ruined). The theme behind this round is that even thought both teams have to kill each other, they would have the other enemy (turrets) to keep an eye on. It would require teamwork in order to go through turrets faster ( one holding shield - turrets focusing on him while the others throwing HE etc). Additionally you could add npc monsters, such as zombies (in reasonable amount), it's very entertaining in my opinion fighting with a zombie with a machete and HE (when he's got right amount of HP, of course). All the details and everything actually is up to a administrator, so its just brief idea, which could be improved further on.

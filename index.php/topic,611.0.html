Monthly server-wide player ranks reset?
« on: June 26, 2014, 15:43:27 »
^this popped into my mind while playing a few minutes on the server, wondering how long it would take to climb up one rank, from 64 to 63.
I have been almost completely inactive during the last few months (or is it a year already?), and yet I am still holding rank 64. Which means, a new player, ever if s/he played with godmode activated activated, has no chance to ever reach one of the upper ranks, unless s/he decides to dedicate his/her entire freetime to cs2d.

Now you obviously have to build up your rank  over time, but it is completely unfair that "dead accounts" are blocking rungs on the ladder to rank 1.

Therefore I suggest a monthly rank reset and a script that safes your "total" rank, which will also be displayed in chat when a player calls up his/her rank.

This way the very active/skilled players would still have a chance to hold the ranks they deserve, while dinosaurs (like me) would still have their ancient ranks saved and be able to share their hoary glory with the newer top ranked rookies.

Opinions, please?

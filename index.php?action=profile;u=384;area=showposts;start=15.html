General Discussion / Re: How did you discover CS2d
« on: July 20, 2016, 13:26:52 »
When i was a kid, my dad bought us our first computer but it wasn't new already. It was a used Pentium II with awful specifications that couldn't run normal games. At that time, my friends were already enjoying CS 1.6 and i couldn't even feel what it's like to play such games.
So, my best friend tried to help me out by finding some weak ass games.
Once, he stumbled upon Counter-Strike 2D and immediatelly told me about it, it was the year 2008. I downloaded it and i couldn't be happier. We played primitive rounds against bots, sometimes joining servers but we often got our asses kicked.
Then, my friend got a better PC and stopped playing 2D while i was improving myself.. (i just couldn't afford a normal PC, fml). During the first year i was playing Zombie Mode a lot and i even got rank 1 on a very popular server at that time - XCafe.net or sth like that. Then i switched to standart. Improved my skill a lot, game tactics, strategy, learned some tricks etc.
In 2010 i got a laptop (i'm still using it). At that time it was a pretty good computer, core i3, 3Gb RAM, 2.13Ghz, 1Gb video... My dad played Crysis 2 on it, but i just continued playing CS2D because.. i fell in love with that game. I made a lot of friends, people recognize me, it was a fun experience. And yet, i still try to launch it at least twice a day.

*wipes tears*
*continues to cry*

Man, i trully love this game.
